# HenryCo business-wow + operational maturity pass

This pass focuses on making existing intelligence, trust, and operational rigor visibly premium across user, staff, and owner surfaces without destabilizing hardened backends.

## Visible-value audit

### Make visible now
- Account Action Center prioritization and source context were already computed but under-explained in UI.
- Trust tier logic and restrictions existed, but users lacked a plain-language trust-state guide.
- Staff priority logic existed, but dashboard/support urgency framing was too implicit.
- Owner briefing logic existed in `owner-data` but was not surfaced in the command page.

### Polish for premium impact
- Recommendation explanation copy now emphasizes relevance and confidence without exposing internal reason codes.
- Support experience now includes reassurance and clear "what happens next" messaging.
- Operations surfaces now expose queue focus and refresh confidence more explicitly.

### Connect to real surface
- Wired owner briefing (`headline`, `focus`, comms health, next steps) into owner command center.
- Wired support queue urgency snapshots into account and staff support surfaces.
- Wired trust restriction visibility directly into account security.

### Defer to avoid noise
- No decorative animations or fake real-time counters.
- No new speculative AI badges or synthetic dashboards.
- No schema rewrites or risky backend flow changes in this pass.

## Safety notes

- No role boundary logic was loosened.
- No trust scoring or enforcement logic was weakened.
- No webhook/cron/auth hardening behavior was modified.
